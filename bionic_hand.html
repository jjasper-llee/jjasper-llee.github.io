Engineering the Hand (Electronics)

As humans, we do everything with our hands. As one who is intrigued by mechanisms, controls and electronics, I wanted to make my own bionic hand that responded and mimicked our very motions.

  • Microcontroller: Teensy 4.0
  • AS560 Absolute Magnetic Encoder
  • ESCs: LIBEE 20A Favourite
  • Radio: Spektrum Receiver & Transmitter
  • Materials: Bambu Lab PAHT-CF, Teflon

Final Notes / Next Steps

My next steps for this project would be to integrate the electronics into the mechanical design. As this is simply CAD work, and doesn't involve learning anything substantial, I decided to put a pause on this. With the working electronics and mechanics for the bionic hand, this to me is already a working prototype (just not assembled into a familiar form).
