About

I’m a mechanical engineer focused on building systems that merge robotics, biomechanics, and flight. My work explores how engineering precision and design intuition can come together to produce mechanisms that are both functional and expressive.

With a background in mechanical design and prototyping, I work across concept development, CAD modeling, actuation systems, and dynamic mechanism optimization. I’m particularly interested in control systems for bionic and aerial robotics.
