Each instrument lives inside its own SPV.

Frequently asked questions

Is this regulated?

We leverage SPVs with Reg D / Reg CF exemptions, audited annually, and serviced by a registered transfer agent.

What is the voting power?

One share equals one vote. Quorum is 25% of outstanding shares before a residency is confirmed.

How are returns generated?

Performance fees, streaming royalties, museum partnerships, and appreciation on eventual resale.

Can I sell my shares?

A secondary bulletin board is being piloted with 30-day liquidity windows and issuer buybacks.
