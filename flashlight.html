Custom Flashlight

Machining my own heavy-duty aluminim flashlight

Background/Origin

As a part of course 2.670, I made my own flashlight out of cylindrical and rectangular aluminum stock. This simple device took a lot of machining and time, certainly proof for why CNC machines are so much more useful.

Engineering Aluminum Ski Poles

The top square-like part is the flashlights head. To make this, I went through a series of steps including facing, drilling, tapping etc. on the mill to create holes for the LED and LED securing screw. As for the handle, that was done on the lathe; steps included turning, facing, chamfering, knurling etc.

Final Notes / Next Steps

As a hands-on person, this was a fun way for me to "get my hands dirty." Cutting, shearing, grinding metal was all part of the fun!
